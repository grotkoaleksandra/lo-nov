The Artist

About

Grotka is a visual artist working across painting, printmaking, and illustration, exploring themes of light, nature, and quiet moments.

With a focus on color and texture, each piece aims to capture a feeling rather than a scene — inviting the viewer to pause and find their own meaning in the work.

Available for commissions, collaborations, and exhibitions worldwide.

Based in Warsaw, Poland
